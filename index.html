TW to GCal

Visit your club's TicketWeb calendar page and click the link. You will see a prompt from Google to authorize this app to manage your calendars.

Once you grant permission, you may select an existing calendar to add events or create a new calendar.

Click "Import all" to copy all of your club's TicketWeb events for the visible month to the selected Google calendar, where users can subscribe and see your events next to their appointments on their smartphone.
